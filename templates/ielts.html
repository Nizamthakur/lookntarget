Test pattern

{% comment %}

The test consists of four parts: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. In the Listening section, there are four sections to assess your ability to understand spoken English. It lasts for about 30 minutes. The Reading section includes three passages that you need to read and comprehend. You will have 60 minutes to complete this section. In the Writing section, you will be given two tasks to complete within 60 minutes. These tasks may include writing an essay or summarizing information. The Speaking section involves an interview that lasts for 10-15 minutes. You will be evaluated on your ability to communicate orally in English and express your thoughts clearly. Overall, the test evaluates your listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills in English through a variety of tasks and formats.
